About

Our Bedwars server offers unique maps, challenging gameplay, and a friendly community. Perfect for both new and experienced players.

How to Play

  1. Join the server using the IP: bedwars.example.com
  2. Select a team and spawn into the game.
  3. Protect your bed at all costs while trying to destroy the beds of other teams.
  4. Collect resources, upgrade your gear, and use strategy to outplay your opponents.
  5. The last team with a bed standing wins!
